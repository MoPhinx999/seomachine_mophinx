### Thermal Cluster Support Content
- Thermal vs insulated curtains explained
- Winter energy comfort use cases

### Soundproof Cluster Support Content
- Acoustic vs soundproof terminology education
- Realistic noise-reduction expectation guides
- Heavy fabric and layer explanations

- Header-style decision content for high-volume terms (`pinch pleat drapes`)
- Extra-long/extra-wide fitting guides
- Floor-to-ceiling and traverse rod implementation guides

### Outdoor Curtains Cluster Support Content
- Patio/pergola/screened-porch use-case pages
- Waterproof and weather resistance selection guides
- Outdoor privacy and UV-protection comparison content

### Sun Shade Sail Cluster Support Content
- Shade sail shape and size selection guides

- Head-to-head comparison: Tracks vs. Standard Rods for heavy drapes
