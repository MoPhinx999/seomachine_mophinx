## Internal Linking Best Practices

1. **Link naturally**: Only add links that directly help the reader’s next decision.
2. **Use mixed anchors**: Rotate contextual anchor phrasing; avoid repetitive exact-match spam.
3. **3-6 links per post**: Use 3-6 strategic internal links in standard blog posts.
4. **Link deep, not generic**: Prefer specific guides/collections over homepage-only links.
5. **Early contextual link**: Include at least one useful internal link in first third of article.
6. **Policy links when needed**: For purchase-risk topics, link shipping/refund policies directly.
