# TheHues Features & Benefits

This document outlines TheHues's key features, benefits, and differentiators to inform content creation that drives conversions and customer acquisition.

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. **Made-to-Measure Curtains & Shades**
- **Feature**: Custom sizing and configuration across curtains, shades, and outdoor products.
- **Benefit**: Customers avoid the "almost fits" problem of standard-size window treatments.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Get a fit that looks intentional, not improvised."

### 2. **Functional Performance Options**
- **Feature**: Blackout, thermal-insulated, sound-focused, and room-darkening options (including 4-layer performance products).
- **Benefit**: Better sleep, privacy, energy comfort, and calmer interior spaces.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Choose one product that handles style, light, temperature, and noise."

### 3. **Customization Depth**
- **Feature**: Multiple header styles, panel setup options, lining options, optional memory shape training, tiebacks, and add-ons.
- **Benefit**: Buyers can tune final look and operation to their exact window and usage needs.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Customize details that change daily usability, not just appearance."

### 4. **Visualization Tool**
- **Feature**: Guided online tool to preview fabric, style, and setup choices before purchase.
- **Benefit**: Reduces uncertainty in online custom ordering.
- **Conversion Angle**: "See your custom setup before checkout."

### 5. **Free Design Service**
- **Feature**: Room-photo mock-up service with expert recommendations and stated 2-4 business day turnaround.
- **Benefit**: Customers get confidence in color/style decisions without design guesswork.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Get a professional preview of your room before you buy."

### 6. **Measurement & Installation Support**
- **Feature**: Product-specific measurement guides (curtains, shades, sun shade sails) and support resources.
- **Benefit**: Fewer ordering errors and better install outcomes.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Measure correctly once, install smoothly once."

### 7. **Trade Program**
- **Feature**: Trade-focused support, trade discounts, dedicated representative service, and no minimum order requirement.
- **Benefit**: Interior designers and trade buyers can source with project efficiency.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Scale your projects with custom sourcing support that acts like part of your team."

### 8. **Broad Product Ecosystem**
- **Feature**: Curtains, shades, outdoor curtains, sun shade sails, hardware, accessories, and related home textiles.
- **Benefit**: Easier one-stop sourcing across interior and outdoor projects.
- **Conversion Angle**: "Build cohesive spaces without stitching together multiple vendors."

### 9. **Custom + Support Workflow**
- **Feature**: Help center, FAQ, order tracking page, and pre-sale guidance channels.
- **Benefit**: Buyers can self-serve or escalate to support during the purchase journey.
- **Conversion Angle**: "From first measurement to delivery tracking, help is built in."

## Technical Features

### Product Configuration
- **Custom dimensions**: Made-to-order width and length selection by product.
- **Header choices**: Multiple heading systems, including pleated and soft-top styles.
- **Panel setup**: One-panel vs two-panel choices in customization workflow.
- **Lining strategy**: Functional liner selection for light/privacy/insulation.
- **Optional enhancements**: Memory shape training, matching tiebacks, heavy-duty sandbags (where supported).

- **100% blackout options**: Light-blocking collections and product variants.
- **Thermal-focused options**: Designed for insulation and comfort.
- **Sound-focused options**: Multi-layer products for noise management scenarios.

## Common Questions & Objections

### "Are custom curtains better than ready-made?"
**Answer**: For non-standard windows and design-specific outcomes, custom sizing and options generally deliver better fit and visual proportion than standard sizes.

### "Can I change or cancel after ordering?"
**Answer**: Current policy states a 12-hour window for cancellation/modification, with fees potentially applying after that window.

### "Will these really help with light, insulation, or noise?"
**Answer**: TheHues offers dedicated functional categories (blackout, thermal, sound-focused) and multi-layer options designed around those outcomes.

### "How do I avoid ordering the wrong setup?"
**Answer**: Start with measurement guides, then validate fabric/style choices through swatches, visualization, or free design service.

### "Is trade buying supported?"
**Answer**: Yes. The trade page states dedicated support, discount structure, and no minimum order requirement.

## Content Creation Guidelines

When writing about TheHues features:

1. **Lead with outcomes**: Start with what changes for the customer (sleep/privacy/comfort/style), then explain product mechanics.
2. **Use room-specific scenarios**: Bedroom, living room, nursery, wide windows.
3. **Reference support assets**: Measurement guides, visualization tool, design service, FAQ.
4. **Set realistic expectations**: Especially for custom-order policies and functional performance claims.
5. **Map CTA to stage**: Learn -> visualize -> swatch -> customize -> checkout.
6. **Use verified site claims only**: Do not add unverified company/legal details.
7. **Keep US-market context**: English-first, US-home terminology and practical examples.
